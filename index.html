I Am
Sonic

A Super-Fast Hedgehog






Hi!

I am the protagonist of the Sonic the Hedgehog video game series published by Sega, as well as numerous spin-off comics, animations, and other media. I am a blue anthropomorphic hedgehog who can run at supersonic speeds and curl into a ball, primarily to attack enemies. I call this move Spin Attack. In most games, I must race through levels, collecting power-up rings and avoiding obstacles and enemies.

I am a one of the world's best-known video game characters and a gaming icon. My series are sold more than 80 million copies by 2011. In 2005, I was one of the first game character inductees into the Walk of Game alongside Mario, Link, and Master Chief.
